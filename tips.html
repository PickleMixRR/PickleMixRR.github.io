Tips

Future updates to this page will include tips, tricks, and more.

For mixed groups, the PickleMixRR app emphasizes playing games within each gender and across each gender whenever possible. For rankings, the females players are ranked against other females and the male players are ranked against other males. The event concludes with an optional Gold Medal Game which pairs the top players in each group.
