PickleMixRR

Pickleball • Mixer • Round Robin

The PickleMixRR app was developed to quick start small group pickleball competitive sessions with equitable rotations over a few hours of play. It can easily accomodate play with varying numbers of male and female players. For mixed groups, it emphasizes playing games within each gender and across each gender whenever possible. For rankings, the females players are ranked against other females and the male players are ranked against other males. The event can conclude with a Gold Medal Game which pairs the top players in each group for a mixed doubles match.
